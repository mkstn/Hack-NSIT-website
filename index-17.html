About Hack@NSIT

Hack@NSIT 2016 is the second iteration of NSIT's very own Annual Software Hackathon
For 24 intense hours, more than 150 of the most talented students from around the world will push the limits of technology.
We bring the most influential mentors, unlimited coffee, wifi, food and an adrenaline fueled environment powered by some of the world’s best companies
